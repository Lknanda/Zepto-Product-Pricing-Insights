# Zepto-Product-Pricing-Insights
Insights on Zepto’s product data: strong focus on groceries and essentials, frequent discounts indicating competitive pricing, mostly stable stock with occasional gaps, and high variety in core categories. Overall, Zepto targets value- and convenience-driven customers.

Overview-
This project explores a dataset collected from Zepto’s online grocery platform, containing product categories, prices, discounts, weights, and stock information. The aim is to understand how Zepto positions its products through pricing, category variety, and inventory decisions.

What I Did-
Cleaned and prepared the raw dataset for analysis
Performed Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) using Python, pandas, and visualizations
Analyzed product distribution, discount patterns, and stock availability
Identified insights on pricing strategy, product variety, stock trends, and demand–supply gaps

Key Insights-
Essentials like groceries, dairy, and packaged foods dominate the catalogue
High discount rates suggest a competitive, value-driven pricing model
Occasional out-of-stock items highlight demand–supply mismatches
Fruits & Vegetables and Cooking Essentials show strongest category depth
Pricing and product spread indicate a focus on value-seeking customers

Conclusion-
Insights About Zepto from This Dataset
1.Product Variety: Zepto has a strong focus on essential categories like groceries, dairy, and packaged food, as shown by high product counts in these segments.
2.Pricing Strategy: Significant discounts are applied on many items, especially in certain categories, suggesting a competitive or volume-driven pricing model.
3.Stock Management: While most items are in stock, the presence of out-of-stock items in some categories could reflect demand-supply mismatches.
4.Category Prioritization: Categories like Fruits & Vegetables and Cooking Essentials dominate in variety, indicating core business strengths.
5.Value Proposition: The combination of aggressive discounts and product spread suggests Zepto is targeting both value-seeking and convenience-driven customers.

This analysis can help Zepto optimize inventory, pricing, and category focus while improving customer satisfaction.
